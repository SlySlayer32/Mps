## 1. HOME PAGE (Landing Page) Content

**Subheadline**: "From move-out to move-in, we handle every detail so you don't have to."

7. **Window Cleaning** - "Crystal clear windows inside and out"
8. **Minor Maintenance** - "Small repairs, painting, and handyman services"

### Why Choose Us Section
**Section Title**: "Why Melbourne Trusts Merlin Property Services"

**Key Points** (3 columns):
1. **Complete Move-Out Service**
   - "We don't just clean interiors. We handle gardens, repairs, and everything needed for a successful bond return or property sale."

2. **100% Guarantee**

## 2. SERVICE PAGE TEMPLATE (Content for Each Service)

### Structure for All Service Pages

#### Hero Section
- Service name as H1
- Brief description (1-2 sentences)
- Key benefit badge (e.g., "100% Bond Back Guarantee" for end of lease)
- CTA: "Get a Quote" button

#### What's Included Section
- Bullet point list of specific tasks/areas covered
- Be detailed and specific to set clear expectations

#### Pricing Section
- **Transparent pricing table or calculator**
- Different property sizes/configurations
- Add-on options clearly listed
- "All prices include GST" note
- CTA: "Book Now" or "Request Custom Quote"

#### Our Guarantee Section
- Specific guarantee for that service
- Insurance information
- What happens if customer isn't satisfied

#### Gallery/Before & After (optional but recommended)
- 3-6 images showing results
- Can use stock photos initially, replace with real photos over time

#### FAQs (3-5 questions per service)
- Address common concerns specific to that service

## 3. END OF LEASE CLEANING PAGE (Detailed Example)

### Hero
**H1**: "End of Lease Cleaning - 100% Bond Back Guarantee"
**Description**: "Get your full bond back with our comprehensive end of lease cleaning service. We clean every inch of your property to meet real estate inspection standards."
**Badge**: "✓ 100% Bond Back Guarantee | ✓ Fully Insured"

### What's Included

**Interior Cleaning**:
- Kitchen: Oven, stovetop, rangehood, cupboards (inside & out), benchtops, sink, dishwasher, fridge (if required)
- Bathrooms: Shower, bath, toilet, tiles, mirrors, vanity, exhaust fans
- Living areas: Walls (mark removal), skirting boards, light fixtures, ceiling fans, switches
- Bedrooms: Wardrobes (inside & out), windows, window tracks, blinds, carpets
- Laundry: Washing machine exterior, dryer lint trap, tubs, cabinets
- All floors: Vacuum and mop all hard floors
- All carpets: Professional steam clean or vacuum (steam clean add-on available)

**Exterior Cleaning** (Optional but Recommended):
- Outdoor areas swept and cleaned
- Patio/balcony pressure washed
- Windows cleaned inside and out

**Garden Services** (Add-on):
- Lawn mowing and edging
- Weeding and garden bed tidying
- Green waste removal

**Minor Repairs** (Add-on):
- Patch and paint small holes
- Replace light bulbs
- Fix minor fixtures

### Pricing

| Property Size | Interior Only | Interior + Exterior | Complete Package* |
|---------------|---------------|---------------------|-------------------|
| 1 Bedroom Apartment | $220 | $280 | $380 |
| 2 Bedroom Apartment | $280 | $360 | $480 |
| 2 Bedroom House | $320 | $420 | $560 |
| 3 Bedroom House | $380 | $500 | $680 |
| 4 Bedroom House | $450 | $590 | $820 |

*Complete Package includes interior, exterior, garden service, and minor repairs (up to 2 hours)

**Add-ons**:
- Carpet steam cleaning: From $50 per room
- Garage/shed cleaning: $80-150
- Additional garden time: $45/hour
- Wall washing: $90 per room

**Note**: Prices are estimates. Final quote provided after property inspection or detailed description.

### Our Guarantee

**100% Bond Back Guarantee**: If your property manager requests re-cleaning of any area we've serviced, we'll return and clean it again at no additional cost. This guarantee applies to standard wear and tear - not pre-existing damage or issues outside our scope.

**Fully Insured**: We carry full public liability insurance for your peace of mind.

### Why Choose Our End of Lease Service?

"Most cleaning companies only handle the interior. We understand that real estate inspections check everything - the garden, the gutters, the exterior. That's why we offer a complete move-out package. One call, one company, complete peace of mind."

### FAQs

**Q: How long does an end of lease clean take?**
A: Typically 4-8 hours depending on property size and condition. We can complete most properties in one day.

**Q: Do I need to be present during the cleaning?**
A: No, as long as we have access to the property. We can collect keys from your real estate agent or lockbox.

**Q: What if the real estate agent says something wasn't cleaned properly?**
A: We'll return within 72 hours to re-clean any areas specified at no additional charge under our bond-back guarantee.

**Q: Do you provide a receipt for the bond claim?**
A: Yes, we provide a detailed tax invoice and checklist showing all areas cleaned.

**Q: Can you help with other move-out tasks?**
A: Absolutely! We offer garden maintenance, minor repairs, painting, junk removal, and more to help with your complete move-out.

## Content Writing Guidelines

### Tone & Voice
- **Professional yet friendly**: You're experts, but approachable

- **Confident**: You guarantee your work
- **Helpful**: Focus on solving customer problems

### SEO Keywords to Include
Primary: cleaning services Melbourne, end of lease cleaning, bond cleaning, commercial cleaning, carpet cleaning Melbourne

Use varied CTAs throughout:

## Media Content Recommendations

### Photography Strategy

**Priority 1: Service Documentation Photos**
- Take before/after photos of actual jobs (with customer permission)
- Capture specific services: cleaning equipment, team in action, final results
- Use these to replace stock photos gradually

**Priority 2: Team Photos**
- Professional headshots if you have staff
- Team in uniform at job sites
- Creates trust and humanizes the business

**Priority 3: Stock Photos (Initial Launch)**
- Use high-quality stock photos from Unsplash, Pexels for launch
- Choose realistic, Australian-looking homes (not mansions)
- Diverse representation of properties and spaces

### Video Content (Future Phase)

**Recommended Videos**:
1. "How to prepare for an end of lease clean" (2 minutes)
2. Time-lapse of a cleaning transformation
3. Customer testimonial videos (despite note about not wanting testimonials on main pages, video testimonials can be powerful on social media)
4. "Meet the team" introduction video

**Where to use**: Embed on relevant service pages, share on social media, use in email marketing.

## Content Migration to WordPress

### Recommended Content Blocks/Sections

1. **Hero Block**: Large background image/illustration + headline + CTA
2. **Services Grid**: Repeatable card component
3. **Two-Column Text + Image**: For "Why Choose Us" sections
4. **Pricing Tables**: Structured table component with highlighting
5. **Process/Steps**: Visual timeline for "How It Works"
6. **FAQ Accordion**: Collapsible Q&A sections
7. **Contact Form**: Custom form with validation
8. **Testimonial Slider**: For future use when you collect reviews
9. **Before/After Comparison**: Slider for showing results

### Content Entry Best Practices

- Use heading hierarchy properly (H1 once per page, then H2, H3)
- Add alt text to all images for SEO and accessibility
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences maximum)
- Use bullet points for scannable content
- Include internal links between related services
- Add schema markup for local business SEO

## Accessibility Considerations

- All images must have descriptive alt text
- Color contrast meets WCAG AA standards minimum
- Font sizes readable on mobile (16px minimum for body text)
- Forms have clear labels and error messages
- Clickable elements are large enough (44×44px minimum)
- Navigation works with keyboard only
