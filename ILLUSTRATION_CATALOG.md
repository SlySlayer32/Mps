# Illustration Catalog for Merlin Property Services

This document organizes cleaning and property services illustrations by their intended use case on the website.

## Folder Structure

```
illustrations/
├── hero/              # Large hero section illustrations
├── service-cards/     # Icons and illustrations for service cards
├── how-it-works/      # Step-by-step process illustrations
├── about/             # About section illustrations
├── icons/             # Small icons for UI elements
└── backgrounds/       # Background patterns and decorative elements
```

## Primary Source: Pixabay
**Best resource found:** https://pixabay.com/illustrations/search/cleaning/
- 2,516+ free cleaning illustrations available
- High quality, royalty-free
- Vector and PNG formats available

---

## 1. HERO SECTION ILLUSTRATIONS (/hero)

### Purpose:
- Large, eye-catching illustrations for landing page hero sections
- Show cleaning professionals in action
- Convey professionalism and friendliness

### Recommended Search Terms on Pixabay:
- "cleaning service illustration"
- "professional cleaner vector"
- "housekeeping illustration"
- "cleaning team"

### What to Look For:
- Full-body illustrations of cleaners with equipment (mop, bucket, broom, spray bottle)
- Blue and orange color schemes (matches examples)
- Friendly, professional character designs
- High resolution (suitable for hero sections)

---

## 2. SERVICE CARDS ILLUSTRATIONS (/service-cards)

### Purpose:
- Smaller illustrations/icons for service category cards
- One illustration per service type

### Required Service Categories:
1. **End of Lease Cleaning** - Search: "house cleaning", "move out cleaning"
2. **Move In Cleaning** - Search: "new home", "house keys"
3. **Commercial Cleaning** - Search: "office cleaning", "commercial building"
4. **Pre-sale Cleaning** - Search: "house sale", "real estate cleaning"
5. **Gardening** - Search: "gardening", "lawn care", "plant care"
6. **Carpet Cleaning** - Search: "carpet cleaning", "vacuum"
7. **Window Cleaning** - Search: "window cleaning", "squeegee"
8. **Commercial/Rental Office Cleaning** - Search: "office space", "desk cleaning"

### Style Guidelines:
- Clean, simple illustrations
- Consistent color palette
- Can be icons or small scene illustrations
- SVG or PNG with transparent backgrounds preferred

## Color Customization

Many sources (especially unDraw and some Pixabay vectors) allow color customization:
- Primary brand color: Orange/Gold (#FF9500 or similar)
- Secondary color: Blue (#0066CC or similar)
- Accent: Light blue/teal for backgrounds
- Ensure consistency across all illustrations
